Discussion-Based Assessment Rubric

Criteria Excellent Good Needs Improvement

Content

24–30 pts.

Clearly shows understanding of course content. Provides sufficient supporting evidence when needed.

21–23 pts.

Somewhat shows understanding of course content. Provides some supporting evidence when needed.

0–20 pts.

Minimally shows understanding of course content. Provides little supporting evidence when needed.

Application

12–14 pts.

Clearly makes connections to other relevant ideas, concepts, texts, and/or real-world examples, as appropriate.

10–11 pts.

Somewhat makes connections to other relevant ideas, concepts, texts, and/or real-world examples, as appropriate.

0–9 pts.

Minimally makes connections to other relevant ideas, concepts, texts, and/or real-world examples, as appropriate.

Preparation

2 pts.

Clearly demonstrates preparation (notes, questions, etc.).

1 pt.

Somewhat demonstrates preparation (notes, questions, etc.).

0 pts.

Minimally demonstrates preparation (notes, questions, etc.).

Participation

2 pts.

Fully participates in the conversation.

1 pt.

Somewhat participates in the conversation.

0 pts.

Minimally participates in the conversation.

Commitment

2 pts.

Initiates and completes the call in a timely manner.

1 pt.

Completes the call in a timely manner.

0 pts.

Does not complete the call in a timely manner.
